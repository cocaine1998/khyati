Our Story

Established with a vision to connect global markets, KHYATI OVERSEAS HUB PVT. LTD. has grown into a premier import-export partner. We specialize in sourcing high-quality goods and ensuring seamless logistics across borders. What started as a small trading initiative has expanded into a comprehensive global network, driven by the increasing demand for reliable, transparent, and efficient trade solutions.

Our dedication to transparency and speed has made us a trusted name in international trade. We believe in fostering long-term relationships with both our suppliers and clients, ensuring that every transaction adds value and drives mutual growth. At KHYATI OVERSEAS, we don't just move goods; we move businesses forward.
